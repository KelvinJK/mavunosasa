Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A future where every smallholder farmer has the resources, knowledge, and opportunity to thrive and build sustainable livelihoods.

Mission

To empower smallholder farmers by providing access to agricultural inputs, training, financial services, and market linkages that enable them to increase productivity and improve their quality of life.

"Before MavunoSasa, I only got 5 bags per acre. Now I get 14. All my children attend good schools and life has completely changed."

Rice Farmer, Morogoro

"Mafunzo ya kilimo bora yamenisaidia sana. Sasa natumia mbegu bora na mbolea kwa wakati. Mazao yangu yameongezeka mara mbili na ninapata bei nzuri sokoni."

"The modern farming training helped me greatly. Now I use quality seeds and timely fertilizer. My yields have doubled and I get fair prices at market."

Maize Farmer, Dodoma
